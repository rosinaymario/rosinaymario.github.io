Dear friends, we have been gathered here today to take photos til your phone's battery is dead.

Not really, right? Exactly.

Nowadays it is almost impossible to attend to a historical-emotional event without taking seven hundred thirty eight thousand photos of the baptized's snot, the second cousin's dress or the comany's dinner dish. From here, we want to issue an appeal to the whole world so no one feels obliged to picture the bride's pimple of the great ocassion (which will keep us company for sure). For two main reason, namely:

  1. We have SUPERPHOTOS and SUPERVIDEO assured: there are peoplo who dreams with their wedding dress or with the church. I am a bit weird and dreamt with the photos: the light, the aesthetics, the background...

    After an exhaustive market research we chose with great joy Jaire, Cris and Nico (you can learn their faces (right to left) on the picture above). We don't have any doubt that the team of great artist that we have hired is more than capable to portray every detail of this great day.

    That means, on one hand, that you can relax (the superphoto of the moment is not your responsability) and, on the other hand, that, since it seems probable that the professional photographer does it a bit better than you üòò, maybe it's a good idea that your phone does not appear in his photo.

  2. It is our big day and we want to look you in the EYES instead of in the lens. When I walk down the aisle to the altar, if possible, I'd rather see your emotion and tears instead of your motion sensor and viewers.

Ea! That said: once the party begins, we know that we cannot lack selfies from each table, funny faces with the bride, in honor of the open barm the video of the first dance... So everyone, download the app now and sharing is living!
